FAQs

How many hostels are there in MAIT ?

The Institute maintains Boy's Hostel having four Blocks on campus. Hostel has over 40 single seater, 65 double seater and 7 four seater rooms

Is there any girl's Hostel in MAIT ?

No, there is no girl's hostel inside the campus.

What is the capacity of your hostel ?

The Capacity of hostel is above 180 students.

Are there A/C and Non A/C rooms for hostellers ?

Yes, we have both A/C and Non A/C rooms.

What is the sharing capacity of rooms in hostels ?

We have single seater, two seater and four seater rooms in the hostel.

How many meals are provided ?

Four meals, which includes Breakfast, Lunch, Evening Snacks and Dinner.

What is the process of allotment of seat ?

Allotment is on the first cum first serve basis.

What types of facilities do you provide to the hostellers ?

Facilities include 24x7 electricity, Wifi Internet services, Gym and indoor games along with other facilities.

What type of basic infrastructure has been
provided to the hostellers ?

Each room is equipped with basic furniture like cot, pillow, chair, table, and cupboard, tube light and fan.

Do you have laundry facility in the hostel ?

Yes we have Laundry facility in the Hostel.

What about ragging ?

Ragging, physical violence, negative discrimination, consumption of alcoholic or narcotics substances, non-vegetarian food is strictly prohibited in the hostel.
